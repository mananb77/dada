# **Dada's Life Timeline**

## **Early Life (1941-1947)**

**February 13, 1941**

* Born in Beawar, Rajasthan  
* Birthplace: Diggi Mohalla  
* Location: 180 kilometers from Jaipur

**1944-1945 (Age 3-4)**

* Earliest childhood memories  
* Lived in a house on Bhargava Street  
* Neighbor: Mahesh Prasad Bhargava (Dada's namesake)  
* Other neighbors included Kaushal Kishore Bhargava, Ram Kishore Bhargava  
* Remember playing with pigeons and flying kites on the terrace

**August 15, 1947 (Age 6\)**

* Indian Independence Day  
* Witnessed the partition \- trains arriving from Pakistan with Hindu refugees  
* Memorable incident at Kharwa: A dacoit named Mor Singh stopped a vehicle from Ajmer  
* Separated Hindus and Muslims in line  
* Dada's elder uncle (Bua's son/Doctor Gudda's Chacha) was on that vehicle  
* Mor Singh, who had eaten salt at Diwan Bahadur Pyarelal's house, helped the uncle reach home safely  
* This loyalty was because of his connection to Dada's maternal uncle Mahesh Prasad Bhargava

## **Education Years**

**Age 6-7 onwards**

* Raised pigeons in the city  
* Various childhood activities and games

**Later Education**

* Studied commerce  
* Attended Commerce College  
* Completed B.Com  
* Completed M.Com degree  
* Principal of the college was a family member  
* Had a good reputation in college

## **Career Journey**

**Early Career**

* Started service/job  
* Lived in various locations during service  
* Lived in building opposite Durlabh Ji Hospital (Sharma Ji's building) for 6 months  
* Lived in Bhargava's building (Dr. Rishi's Chacha's building) for about 4 years  
* Moved to 32 Tilak Nagar (opposite LBS) with elder brother and Bhabhi

**Union Bank Joining**

* Influenced by Shyambhave Lal Ji Bhargava (retired as GM of Jaipur Bank)  
* Shyambhave Lal Ji had 11 sons and 1-2 daughters  
* Advised Kailash to join banking instead of other services  
* Convinced by family friend Shyambhave Lal Ji and maternal uncle  
* Attended Union Bank interview  
* Successfully selected for Union Bank  
* Never applied for transfers \- all postings came automatically  
* Philosophy: Helping others brings automatic blessings from God
