# **दादा की बातें — His Words, His Wisdom, His Legacy**

### **Kailash Prasad Bhargava | Born: 13 February 1941, Beawar, Rajasthan**

---

## **🕊️ On Sacrifice & Family**

*"उस सैक्रिफाइस का ये फायदा रहा कि तीन परिवारों का उनको मिला — उनका नाम भी रहा।"*

*"That sacrifice bore fruit — he gained the belonging of three families, and his name lived on in all of them."*

---

*"जो लेने में ही बिलीव करते हैं उनका भगवान बहुत बुरा करता है।"*

*"Those who only believe in taking — God does not spare them."*

---

## **🌱 On Hard Work & Self-Reliance**

*"मैं करूंगा — कोई बात नहीं पापा, मैं तो करूंगा।"*

*"I will do it — it doesn't matter, Papa, I will find a way."*

*(Said when his father refused to send him to Ajmer for studies, believing there was no money.)*

---

*"भगवान अपने आप देगा। मेरे तो ये शिव जी की कृपा है। आ जाएंगे पैसे कहीं से।"*

*"God will provide on His own. This is Shiva's grace upon me. Money will come from somewhere."*

---

*"एक्सरसाइज हो जाती है मेरी।"*

*"It's my exercise."*

*(His answer when people asked why he walked 15 km every day instead of taking the bus — because he couldn't afford the 4 paisa fare.)*

---

## **🤝 On Doing Good for Others**

*"दूसरे का भला करने से क्या भला होता है — वो मैं बतलाना चाह रहा हूं।"*

*"What good comes from doing good for others — that is what I want you to understand."*

*(Said after recounting how teaching his landlord's children led to free rent and home-cooked meals being sent to his door.)*

---

*"जीवन के अंदर दूसरों का भला चाहो, उसका भला अपने आप भगवान करता है।"*

*"In life, wish well for others — God takes care of the rest Himself."*

*(His life's central philosophy, said after reflecting on never having to apply for a job — every opportunity came to him.)*

---

## **💼 On His Career**

*"सारी चीजें आई हैं — मैंने कोई चलाके अप्लाई नहीं किया। ऑटोमेटिक।"*

*"Everything came to me — I never chased or applied for anything. It all came automatically."*

*(Reflecting on how every job in his life found him, not the other way around.)*

---

*"कॉलेज से निकलते ही सेकंड डे मेरी सर्विस पक्की थी।"*

*"The very next day after my last college exam, my appointment was confirmed."*

---

## **🏋️ On His Childhood & Health**

*"खूब दूध पिया, खूब मक्खन खाया, खूब घी पिया है।"*

*"I drank milk freely, ate butter freely, drank ghee freely."*

*(On growing up on a farm with cows, buffaloes and 13 goats — a childhood of wholesome abundance.)*

---

*"नींद इतनी आती थी कि एक बाल्टी भर के पानी की डालते थे — फिर भी नहीं जगते थे। फिर दूसरी बाल्टी।"*

*"I slept so deeply that one full bucket of cold water wouldn't wake me. Then a second bucket."*

*(On how hard physical play and sport exhausted him as a boy.)*

---

## **🇮🇳 On Witnessing History — Partition, 1947**

*"उस जमाने में हिंदू मुस्लिम जो झगड़े हुए थे — ये एक बड़ा किस्सा है जिसको कि कभी जिंदगी में भूल नहीं सकते।"*

*"The Hindu-Muslim conflicts of those times — this is one story I can never forget as long as I live."*

*(Recounting the day a dacoit named Mor Singh protected Hindu passengers on a train, sparing them because of the honour he owed Dada's great-grandfather, Diwan Bahadur Pyarelal ji.)*

---

## **👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 On the Bhargava Family Legacy**

*"सेवन पीढ़ी की नॉलेज रहे — दादा के दादा के दादा — ये सब तुम्हारी नॉलेज में रहे।"*

*"Let the knowledge of seven generations remain with you — grandfather's grandfather's grandfather — all of them should live in your memory."*

*(His parting words, urging the family to preserve the lineage and remember where they came from.)*

---

## **✨ The Thread That Runs Through His Life**

Dada never applied for a single job. Every opportunity walked to him. He walked 15 km a day rather than spend 4 paisa. He taught children for free and received food and rent in return. He sacrificed comfort again and again — and watched abundance return to him.

His life is a quiet, living proof of the one thing he kept saying:

*"दूसरों का भला चाहो — भगवान अपने आप देगा।"* *"Wish well for others — God will take care of you Himself."*

---

*Recorded in conversation with his grandchildren, 2023\.* *"इतनी स्टोरी है कि 10 रोज तक अपन डिस्कस करें तो खत्म नहीं हुए।"* *"There are so many stories, we could talk for 10 days and still not finish."*
